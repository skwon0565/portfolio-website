    My Journey to Coding

    Hello World! My life journey took a wide U-turn when I transitioned from being an academic student pursuing a career in physical therapy to becoming a Software Developer immersed in the world of technology. However, it wasn't a simple change; rather, it was a roller coaster filled with thinking, planning, and personal growth.

    In college, I excelled in my studies, ranking among the top students in my doctorate program. I was always seeking new opportunities in my field but eventually came to a realization that I wasn't finding fulfillment in this profession.
